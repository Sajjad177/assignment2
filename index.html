Our Popular Tours

Everest is our most popular. We send 100-200 people there every year.Why tourists want to visit Everest base camp?That is popular question now a day.

  • Mount Everest, the highest mountain in the world, which is known as "Sagarmatha" in Nepali means “forehead of sky”, the goddess mother of the world
  • You can climb Kalapatter for best view and visit the Sherpa villages call Namche, Khumjung, Konde, Pangboche.All over the world that are planning to spend their holiday in Nepal mostly visit Everest.

The cost is depending on your habit of expense, the cold and hot drinks are slightly expensive. The also depend on group size and hotel categories. The cost start from US$ 100 per day per person includes your flight ticket. You need two permits one is Sagarmatha National Park permit and another is TIMS permit. TIMS permit for Everest is no need until another notice but you need to pay local permission permit similar like TIMS which provide by local government in Lukla.
